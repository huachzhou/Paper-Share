Macro Graph Neural Networks

For Online Billion-Scale Recommender Systems

Abstract

Predicting Click-Through Rate (CTR) in billion-scale recommender systems poses a long-standing challenge for Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) due to the overwhelming computational complexity involved in aggregating billions of neighbors. This paper introduces Macro Graph Neural Networks (MacGNN), a revolutionary approach to CTR prediction designed for billion-scale recommendation systems.

Model Overview

Key Contributions

  • Introduced a macro recommendation graph that reduces neighbor size from billions to hundreds.
  • Proposed a novel Macro Graph Neural Network (MacGNN) for efficient CTR prediction.
  • Validated the model with extensive offline experiments and real-world A/B testing on Taobao's homepage feed.
